# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers, researchers, and creators who direct an AI agent through complex work involving code, documents, research, planning, files, and visual artifacts. They need to understand what the agent is doing, intervene quickly, and move between conversation, code, runtime state, and spatial work without losing context.

## Product Purpose

AXIOM is an operating environment for visible human-agent collaboration. It turns agent work into inspectable conversations, actions, files, plans, artifacts, and spatial objects instead of hiding the work behind a chat response. Success means users can direct ambitious tasks, follow progress, inspect evidence, and continue the work from any surface with confidence.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, precise, alive.

## Anti-references

AXIOM must not resemble a generic AI chat wrapper, a neon cyberpunk dashboard, a purple-gradient SaaS template, or a decorative whiteboard demo. It should avoid excessive cards, ornamental glow, fake activity, vague status messages, and visual effects that compete with the user's work.

## Design Principles

1. Make real work visible without exposing private chain-of-thought.
2. Keep the interface quiet until state or evidence needs attention.
3. Use familiar professional-tool affordances so users can act without relearning basics.
4. Let every surface share one agent context while preserving the best interaction model for that surface.
5. Prefer direct manipulation, inspectable artifacts, and verified outcomes over narration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast and keyboard access for all primary workflows. Support reduced motion without removing state feedback. Never rely on color alone for status, preserve readable text at compact desktop sizes, and keep controls usable across narrow and wide windows.
